# HBoot-Lab

HBoot-Lab is the personal lab of HBoot.

This repository is the source of truth for all product development logs, independent developer journals, AI experiments, business validation, and content creation.

It is **NOT** a technical knowledge base.

Technical learning notes belong in the **Learning-Blog** repository.

---

## Mission

Record the entire journey of building products.

Not just the final result, but also:

- Ideas
- Decisions
- Experiments
- Failures
- Lessons learned
- User feedback
- Product iterations

Every product should leave a complete development history.

---

## Out of Scope

Do NOT place the following content here:

- Programming tutorials
- Framework documentation
- Algorithm notes
- Language learning notes
- General technical knowledge
- Reading notes

These belong in **Learning-Blog**.

---

## Writing Principles

Write from real experience.

Do not invent stories.

Every article should answer at least one of these questions:

- Why did I build this?
- What problem did I encounter?
- How did I solve it?
- What mistakes did I make?
- What did I learn?
- What will I improve next?

Readers should be able to understand the thinking behind the product, not just the implementation.
